Hello, i'm Helena Kennedy

Welcome to my world. I see my world being invaded by technology that is developing faster than us humans are able to keep up with. These technologies have only taken off in the last couple of decades and I've seen life before and after social media. I want to be part of a refreshing approach to internet and media design that uses all these amazing discoveries to benefit us rather than steal our joy.

Design Innovation

The Bachelor of Design Innovation (BDI) is a broad and creative degree designed to prepare me for the expanding design industry. We are investigating design principles, histories, theories and practices throughout research and creative projects. I hope to gain a solid design foundation and begin to challenge and forge the future of design practice.

  • Core Design Projects

Interaction Design

I am exploring the creation of products and technologies that enable people to achieve objectives. It is one of the newest and fastest growing areas of design. We use strategies, psychology and social/empathetic skills to understand people and the way they interact with the world. We are reimagining the way people experience all kinds of products from websites to apps or even physical systems.

  • UX Projects

Psychology

Exploring the science of psychology gives me an insight into the random and perplexing state of human beings. We explore fascinating ideas and theories about behaviour, devolopment, memory and the mind. These skills give me valuable insight into people, research and ethical practices. I beleive psychology is key to developing meaningful and positively influencial design.
